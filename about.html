Hi, I'm Nina

I am a senior studying Graphic Design and English at Binghamton. I am currently preparing for law school, despite my artistic interest. I often hesitate to refer to myself as an "artist" or "designer", however it is ultimately what I am most passionate about. A lot of my work derives from my love for music, color, and experimental creativity. I have worked with countless mediums and am always looking for new ways to experiment with my creative process. I hope to someday find a way to incorporate my creativity into my work later on.
